Giraffe

Giraffes rarely sleep for long periods. They usually sleep standing up for short intervals, but sometimes they lie down for deeper sleep, though this is quite rare.

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Average Length: 4.3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Savannas

The giraffe is a tall, herbivorous mammal with a long neck and legs, known for its distinctive spotted coat. It is the tallest land animal, found in sub-Saharan Africa, and primarily feeds on the leaves of trees.
